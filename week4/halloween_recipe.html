"A cute and simple little Halloween treat that everyone will love. The amount this recipe makes depends on how many cookies and chocolate kisses you have. It just depends on how many you want. The last time I made these, I ended up making about 140 or so.," says Sarah Smith.

Ingredients

  • 2 (16 ounce) packages fudge stripe cookies
  • 1/4 cup honey, or as needed
  • 1 (9 ounce) bag milk chocolate candy kisses, unwrapped
  • 1 (4.5 ounce) tube black decorating gel

Directions

  1. Place a fudge stripe cookie with bottom side up onto a work surface.
  2. Smear a small dab (about 1/8 teaspoon) of honey onto the bottom of a chocolate kiss
  3. Secure the candy piece to the center of the cookie, covering the hole
  4. Use decorating gel to pipe a small bow onto the cookie at the base of the candy piece
  5. Repeat with remaining ingredients
